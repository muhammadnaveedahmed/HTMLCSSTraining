In the 13th century, Yunus Emre was the cheif Justice of Alnihaan in the state of Anatolia (modern-day Turkiye). Yunus Emre also takes on a spiritual journey in Anatolia, radically changing his life and becoming a dervish. He believes on equal distribution of wealth and justice in all humanbeings to spread peace & love in the whole world through rules of law.
